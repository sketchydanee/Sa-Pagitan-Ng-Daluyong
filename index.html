Sa Pagitan ng Daluyong

by Dane Ezekiel Hablo

A body rises where the water loosens its grip, suspended for a single moment escaping the turbulence. Beneath the surface, countless small fish swim together through the pull of the violent sea, enduring currents far greater than themselves.

This piece reflects on the exhaustion and disillusionment that drive many Filipinos to seek lives elsewhere, questioning what is lost when survival begins to mean leaving home behind.
